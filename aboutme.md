# Richard Lui

## About Me
Dr. Richard Lui (LUI Wing Cheung) serves as a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).
He likes playing badminton.

Email: cswclui@polyu.edu.hk

## Projects and Achievement
Dr. Lui received the Faculty Award in Teaching (Outstanding Individual) in 2016, Merit (Team) in 2022, and Outstanding (Team) in 2023.​
His recent research interest include game based learning and AI in education.

Dr. Richard Lui has been the PI of various UGC-funded projects since 2013 and has extensive experience in teaching using innovative pedagogy, developing simulation games, and creating e-learning platforms. He has published journal and conference papers related to online learning, simulation games, and gamification. Currently, he is leading two UGC-funded projects related to the development of GenAI-powered chatbots and learning platforms. His projects were shortlisted for the QS Reimagined Education Award in 2024 and won the Silver award in 2025 under the category “AI in Education.”
