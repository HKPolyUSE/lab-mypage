Research & Innovation

Research Interests: Game-based learning and AI in education

Dr. Richard Lui has been the PI of various UGC-funded projects since 2013 and has extensive experience in teaching using innovative pedagogy, developing simulation games, and creating e-learning platforms. He has published journal and conference papers related to online learning, simulation games, and gamification.

Currently, he is leading two UGC-funded projects related to the development of GenAI-powered chatbots and learning platforms. His projects were shortlisted for the QS Reimagined Education Award in 2024 and won the Silver award in 2025 under the category "AI in Education."
